Selected Products

  1. Apple's Health App

    Description: Apple's Health App is a comprehensive health monitoring application that provides users with real-time insights into their health data. It allows users to track various health metrics, such as activity, sleep, and overall health data. The app features personalized health goals, medication reminders, and data visualization tools to empower users in managing their overall well-being.

  2. Google Fit

    Description: Google Fit is a wearable fitness tracking platform that monitors users' physical activity, heart rate, and sleep patterns. It offers real-time feedback, activity challenges, and seamless integration with smartphone apps, enabling users to track their fitness goals and progress effortlessly. With its user-friendly interface and comprehensive features, Google Fit promotes an active and healthy lifestyle.

  3. Amazon Echo Show

    Description: Amazon Echo Show is a versatile smart display device that offers various features such as managing smart home devices, Alexa(voice activated control) to manage various applications. Applications such as video or voice calls, other health monitoring apps.

Design Approach and Feature Selection

In the design of our own product, we will prioritize usability, accessibility, and functionality to ensure a seamless user experience for individuals of all backgrounds and technological competencies. Here's what we will and will not do:

What We Will Do:

  • Focus on simplicity and ease of navigation to cater to users with limited technology skills, similar to Apple's Health App.
  • Offer comprehensive health tracking capabilities, including physical activity, medication reminders, and real-time data visualization, inspired by Google Fit and Apple's Health App.
  • Integrate IoT devices for health monitoring to provide users with valuable insights into their well-being, akin to Google Fit and Apple's Health App.
  • Provide personalized insights and recommendations based on collected health data to empower users in managing their health effectively.
  • Include voice command accessibility and large text/high contrast mode for improved accessibility, especially for elderly users or those with visual impairments.
  • Ensure stability and reliability of the application, addressing any technical issues promptly to enhance user satisfaction and trust.
  • Offer seamless data sharing with healthcare providers to facilitate informed decision-making and personalized care plans, similar to the features found in Apple's Health App.

What We Will Not Do:

  • Compromise on user privacy and data security, mitigating any privacy concerns associated with smart devices and data sharing.
  • Overcomplicate the user interface or feature set, aiming for a balance between functionality and simplicity to avoid overwhelming users.
  • Remove features or functionalities without careful consideration, ensuring that updates or changes enhance the user experience rather than detract from it.
  • Rely solely on voice command interaction, acknowledging the importance of offering alternative input methods to accommodate diverse user preferences and accessibility needs.

Features Drawn from Selected Products:

  • Comprehensive Health Tracking (Apple's Health App)
  • Real-time Data Visualization (Google Fit)
  • Integration with IoT Devices (Google Fit and Apple's Health App)
  • Personalized Insights and Recommendations (Apple's Health App)
  • Voice Command Accessibility (Amazon Echo Show)
  • Large Text/High Contrast Mode (Google Fit)
  • Seamless Data Sharing with Healthcare Providers (Apple's Health App)
