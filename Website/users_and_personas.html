Persona Development and Scenarios

Introduction: Purpose of Personas and Scenario in Interaction Design (IxD)

Through research and insights from user surveys, we've crafted fictional representations known as Personas. These Personas serve a vital role in the design process, facilitating empathy with users by delving into their needs, behaviors, and motivations. This deep understanding enables us to make informed design decisions that cater to a diverse range of user requirements.

Personas, alongside scenarios, are instrumental in the Interaction Design (IxD) process. They humanize design endeavors by moving from functional user needs to human-like personas with clear objectives and motivations. By fostering empathy and understanding, Personas and scenarios empower designers to create user-centered and intuitive solutions. Acting as effective communication tools, they ensure that design decisions remain rooted in user insights throughout the product development lifecycle. Through the integration of Personas and scenarios, designers can craft engaging and impactful user experiences tailored to their target audience.

Persona 1: Sarah Thompson

  • Background: Sarah is a 75-year-old retiree living alone. She has recently been diagnosed with hypertension and diabetes, requiring regular monitoring of her vital signs and taking medication.
  • Scenario: Sarah wakes up in the morning and has breakfast, where she receives a notification reminding her to take her morning medications and record her blood pressure and blood glucose levels via connected bluetooth device. She switches on the tv and opens the healthcare monitoring app via voice control to view and navigate her results in user-friendly dashboard, tracking trends and sharing the information with her family and healthcare provider.

Persona 2: David Thompson

  • Background: David is a 40-year-old working professional with a busy schedule and Sarah Thompsons's son who lives in another country.
  • Scenario: During his lunch break, David opens the app to check Sarah's activity and medication schedule. He quickly views Sarah's step count for the day along with other vital signs, such as the frequency of fridge and appliance usage. This routine check provides David with peace of mind, ensuring that his elderly, health-compromised mother is doing well and sticking to her prescribed routine.

Persona 3: Emily Johnson

  • Background: Emily is a 30-year-old nurse working in the community, she is responsible for monitoring the health status of multiple patients simultaneously remotely and providing timely interventions as needed.
  • Scenario: Emily accesses the healthcare monitoring app via a desktop computer in the medical centre. She uses the application to remotely monitor her patients' vital signs and receive real-time alerts for any abnormalities. During her remote consultations via phone call or teleconferencing, Emily relies on the app's dashboard to quickly assess each patient's condition and prioritize her interventions based on the severity of their symptoms.
