Problem Concept

  • Increasing Demand for remote healthcare monitoring.
  • Importance of Interaction Design in Healthcare
  • Focus on Accessibilty, User-Centered Design

The App

  • Utilises Raspberry-Pi to collect data from Internet of Things devices
  • Using Voice-controlled interface connected to TV
  • Ability to also access the app through smart phone

Target Audience

  • Users with disabilites, poor health, chronic conditions
  • Caregivers/Family Members to monitor health needs of others
  • Health Proffesionals can analyse the data to advise adjustments

Apples Health App

  • Track Data from Apple Watch or third-party apps
  • Emergency SOS to allow quickly call for help and notify emergency contacts

Google Fit

  • Setting Personalized goals from tracked data
  • View real-time stats

Amazon Echo Show

  • Voice Activated Interface
  • Set voice assitant reminders
  • Support video call with healthcare proffesionals
